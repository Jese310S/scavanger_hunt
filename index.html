About Game

This is a wizards themed scavenger hunt game intended for those of all ages. Hogwarts is where you want to go, did you get a letter saying so? Unfortunately yor spellbook was lost due to a mix up with Owl Post. You need to find all the spells and complete your spellbook. The wand chooses the wizard, so they say, grab yours to get set on your way!
